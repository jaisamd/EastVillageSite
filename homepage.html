We Love The East Village

The Lower East Side has been a source of inspiration to artists around the world for generations.

an epicenter of artistic creativity and counterculture.

As former residents of this neighborhood from periods ranging from the mid 1990's to today, we are united by a passion to preserve and celebrate the artistic tradition and counter cultural history of the area, which has been rapidly and continually changed by gentrification. To help ensure that the neighborhood’s creative heritage isn’t lost to time, we have gathered information about some of the major figures of important artistic movements generated by the rich community that became possible in the area when comparatively lower rent to income ratio in the area made contemplation and community gathering and experimentation possible.
